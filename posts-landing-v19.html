Frequently asked questions

+What is Posts?
Posts is the new way to communicate directly with your fans. It starts with the email marketing you already rely on and adds web reach, interactivity, and the targeting and gating that come from Openstage fan data. Publish each message to email, the web, or both, complete with scheduling and full analytics.
+Does Posts replace email?
It replaces how you send email, not email itself. Every Post reaches your existing list in the inbox as always, and also publishes to the web where new fans can find it. One tool, one workflow, nothing extra to maintain. Powered by your fan data, Posts helps you deliver better fan experiences that build stronger relationships.
+What can you build?
Tour announcements, first-listen drops, album campaigns, merch exclusives, gated content, polls, pre-save unlocks, early access, community threads, fan acquisition campaigns.
+How is this different from other email marketing tools like Mailchimp?
Mailchimp sends email. Posts sends email and creates a web experience. Your fans can react, comment, vote, share, and sign up all from the same message. Plus your fan data gets richer with every send.
+Do Posts get sent to email automatically?
Every message goes where you decide: email, the web, or both. Each one is its own decision.
