Frequently asked questions

+What is Posts?
Posts is the new way to communicate directly with your fans. It starts with the email marketing you already rely on and adds web reach, interactivity, and the targeting and gating that come from Openstage fan data. Publish each message to email, the web, or both.
+Do Posts get sent to email automatically?
Every message goes where you decide: email, the web, or both. Each one is its own decision.
+What can you build?
Tour announcements, first-listen drops, album campaigns, merch exclusives, gated content, polls, pre-save unlocks, early access, community threads, fan acquisition campaigns.
+How is this different from other email or newsletter tools?
Other tools publish. Openstage publishes on top of your fan data, so you can send to any segment and gate any post or block by who fans are and what they have done. Plus Bandsintown, DSP embeds, pre-save blocks, and tools built for how you release.
+How is this different from Broadcasts?
Posts is how you send email campaigns going forward, now with web reach, interactivity, targeting, and gating built in. Broadcasts remain the tool for SMS and lifecycle flows for now.
+Do fans need an account?
Depends on your settings. Public, gated behind signup, gated to a specific audience, or paid subscribers. Per post and per block.
+What pre-save platforms are supported?
Spotify and Apple Music. YouTube and Amazon Music require linking out.
+How does data collection work?
Every view, click, poll response, comment, and signup flows into your fan data automatically. No exports. No integrations.
+Can I gate individual blocks?
Yes. Lock a specific block behind a signup, a subscription, a custom audience, or a completed action like a pre-save or quiz, while keeping the rest public.
+How do I get started?
Sign up and we will get you set up. New Openstage artists start with Posts from day one.
