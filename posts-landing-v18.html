Frequently asked questions

+What is Posts?
Posts is the new way to communicate directly with your fans. It starts with the email marketing you already rely on and adds everything email was never built to give you: interactivity, fan intelligence, web reach, and the tools artists actually need. Publish each message to email, the web, or both. Every interaction feeds your fan graph. Other tools know subscribers. Openstage knows fans.
+Do Posts get sent to email automatically?
Every message goes where you decide: email, the web, the feed, or any combination. Messages you publish to the web can be discovered and shared by new fans. Each one is its own decision.
+What can you build?
Tour announcements, first-listen drops, album campaigns, merch exclusives, gated content, polls, pre-save unlocks, early access, community threads, fan acquisition campaigns.
+How is this different from other email or newsletter tools?
Posts is connected to your fan graph. Other tools know subscribers. Openstage knows fans. You can target delivery and gate access independently, at the post and block level, by who fans are and what they've done. Plus Bandsintown, DSP embeds, presave blocks, and gating logic built for how you release.
+How is this different from Broadcasts?
Posts is how you send email campaigns going forward, now with web reach, interactivity, and gating built in. Broadcasts remain the tool for SMS and lifecycle flows for now.
+Are Posts replacing Broadcasts?
Posts becomes the home for your email and web campaigns. Broadcasts remain for SMS and lifecycle flows for now.
+Do fans need an account?
Depends on your settings. Public, gated behind signup, gated to a specific audience, or paid subscribers. Per post and per block.
+What presave platforms are supported?
Spotify and Apple Music. YouTube and Amazon Music require linking out.
+How does data collection work?
Every view, click, poll response, comment, and signup flows into your fan graph automatically. No exports. No integrations.
+Can I gate individual blocks?
Yes. Lock a specific block behind a signup, a subscription, a custom audience, or a completed action like a pre-save or quiz, while keeping the rest public. Signup happens in context.
+When can I start?
Posts is in early access. Request access and we'll get you set up. New Openstage artists default to Posts from day one.
